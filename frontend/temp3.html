DURATION OF ACTION

Effective for approximately 24 hours per dose.

STORAGE

Room temperature (20-25°C). Keep away from moisture.

warning

Side Effects

Dry Cough Dizziness Headache Fatigue

Consult your doctor if symptoms persist or worsen.

emergency_home

Overdose Alert

Seek emergency medical attention immediately.

Severe hypotension (very low blood pressure) and electrolyte disturbances can occur. Do not wait for symptoms to appear.

Missed Dose?

Take it as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose. Do NOT take two doses at once.

cancel

Contraindications

  • History of angioedema related to previous ACE inhibitor treatment.
  • Concomitant use with aliskiren in patients with diabetes.
